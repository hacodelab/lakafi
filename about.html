Our Story

Who We Are

Lakafi & Toretto Engineering & Supplies is a growing Tanzanian business dedicated to providing quality engineering products, importation services, and travel solutions to individuals and businesses across Tanzania and Zanzibar.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between East Africa and international markets, we have built strong partnerships with suppliers in China and Hong Kong to bring the best products at the most competitive prices.

From full house finishing packages to visa assistance and safari tours, we are committed to being your one-stop business partner — reliable, transparent, and always focused on your success.
