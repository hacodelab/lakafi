Visa Application Made Easier

We provide professional visa assistance services to help you navigate the application process for China and Europe (Schengen area). Our team guides you through the required documents, forms, and procedures to improve your chances of approval.

⚠️ Important Notice: We provide visa assistance services only. Final visa approval decisions are made solely by the respective embassies and consulates. We do not guarantee visa approval.

Visa Types

Visas We Assist With

🇨🇳

China Visa

Business & Tourist

We assist with the Chinese visa application process for business travelers visiting factories, markets, and trade expos, as well as tourists exploring China.

Documents Required (General):

  • •Valid passport (min. 6 months validity)
  • •Completed visa application form
  • •Passport-size photographs
  • •Return flight booking
  • •Hotel reservation or invitation letter
  • •Bank statement (last 3 months)
🇪🇺

Schengen Visa (Europe)

Business, Tourist & Transit

A Schengen visa allows travel to 27 European countries. We assist you in preparing and submitting a strong visa application with complete documentation.

Documents Required (General):

  • •Valid passport (min. 3 months beyond return date)
  • •Schengen visa application form
  • •Travel insurance (min. €30,000 coverage)
  • •Return flight booking
  • •Hotel/accommodation proof
  • •Bank statement & proof of financial means

📌 Note: Document requirements may vary depending on your specific situation and the embassy you apply to. Our team will provide you with a full, personalised checklist when you contact us.
